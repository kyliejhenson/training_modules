# Audience Profile: Solutions Partners

## Role Summary
External partners who help enterprise customers implement Claude into their workflows. They advise on architecture, guide integrations, and support clients through deployment and adoption. They operate across both technical and business conversations.

## Tags

### Technical Skill Level
Intermediate

### Use Case Types
- Technical implementation
- Security & compliance
- Workflow integration
- Business value & ROI
- Adoption & change management
- Evaluation & testing

### Needs Technical Application Practice
Yes

### Needs Value-Based Selling Information
Yes

## Content Matching Notes
Solutions partners are the most broadly tagged profile — all six use case types apply because they span the full lifecycle from positioning through adoption. They sit at intermediate because they need enough depth to guide implementations but often hand off the deepest technical work to specialists. They need both practice and selling content because they must demonstrate capability and articulate value to their clients simultaneously.

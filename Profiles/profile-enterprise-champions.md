# Audience Profile: Enterprise Champions

## Role Summary
Internal advocates who drive Claude adoption within their organizations. They manage rollout strategy, secure executive buy-in, navigate internal governance, and ensure teams actually use the products once deployed.

## Tags

### Technical Skill Level
Intermediate

### Use Case Types
- Security & compliance
- Workflow integration
- Business value & ROI
- Adoption & change management

### Needs Technical Application Practice
No

### Needs Value-Based Selling Information
Yes

## Content Matching Notes
Enterprise champions need content that helps them justify, govern, and drive usage — not build. They are tagged intermediate because they need to understand what's possible without writing code themselves. They are the only profile without technical implementation or evaluation & testing, and the primary audience for adoption & change management content. Value-based information matters because they are constantly selling Claude internally to leadership and peer teams.

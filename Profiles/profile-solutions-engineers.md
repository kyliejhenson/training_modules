# Audience Profile: Anthropic Solutions Engineers

## Role Summary
Technical experts who help prospects and customers understand, evaluate, and implement Claude products. They bridge product capabilities with customer needs during pre-sale and post-sale engagements.

## Tags

### Technical Skill Level
Advanced

### Use Case Types
- Technical implementation
- Security & compliance
- Workflow integration
- Business value & ROI
- Evaluation & testing

### Needs Technical Application Practice
Yes

### Needs Value-Based Selling Information
Yes

## Content Matching Notes
Solutions engineers need content that goes deep on how products work and how to position them. They are the most broadly tagged technical audience because they must answer both "how do I build with it" and "why should you buy it." They do not need adoption or change management content — that falls to customer-side teams after the sale.

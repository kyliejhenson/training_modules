## Content

Skills can be distributed at different scopes depending on your audience:

- **Project skills**: Commit `.claude/skills/` to version control so everyone on the project has access.
- **Plugins**: Create a `skills/` directory in your plugin to distribute skills as part of a plugin package.
- **Managed**: Deploy organization-wide through managed settings for enterprise-level distribution.

This flexibility lets teams standardize workflows (e.g., a `/deploy` skill in every project) while allowing individual developers to maintain personal productivity skills (e.g., a `/explain-code` skill in their home directory).

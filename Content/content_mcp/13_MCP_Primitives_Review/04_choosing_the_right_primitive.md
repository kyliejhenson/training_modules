## Content

Each MCP primitive is controlled by a different part of your application stack:

| Need | Use |
|------|-----|
| Give Claude new capabilities | **Tools** |
| Get data into your app for UI or context | **Resources** |
| Create predefined workflows for users | **Prompts** |

The key insight is who controls each primitive:
- **Tools** serve the model
- **Resources** serve your app
- **Prompts** serve your users

These are high-level guidelines to help you choose the right primitive for your specific use case.
